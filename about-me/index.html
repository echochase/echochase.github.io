About Me

Here's some stuff about me!

Education

I'm pursuing a double degree in computer science and statistics at UNSW. I'm in my third year and I should graduate by May 2025!

My favourite COMP courses in uni so far are COMP6843 (WebSec) and COMP2041 (Software Construction); I highly recommend taking both as soon as you have the prerequisites. Cybersecurity might be an acquired taste, but 2041 is definitely for everyone.

After I graduate, I'd like to work as a security analyst (pen tester) or a full stack developer.

Tutoring

I currently work as a private tutor for HSC physics and maths. Those were my favourite subjects back in high school, so I'm really passionate about them. It's like being paid to yap for an hour or two.

I used to work for a tutoring company based in Burwood, where I tutored classes part-time and found my love for teaching. I also wrote an entire HSC physics textbook for them, complete with teacher's notes. I remember drawing up a makeshift legal agreement myself for the rights to the textbook, with the help of a law friend of mine. Even though I left when workload got heavy, I really enjoyed my time there. Nowadays I enjoy the freedom of working as a private tutor. But whether it's a public or private tutoring gig, all my students have done really well.
